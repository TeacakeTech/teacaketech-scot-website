We aim to run new monthly events focusing on Technology - programming languages and tools, data and databases, servers and hosting, and more. We will usually have a virtual event with a talk followed by questions. The events will start and end with some conversation in small breakout rooms so you can get to know the community.

Occasionally we'll hold in-person events. For these please be aware of Covid and other infectious illnesses. Do not come if think you may be unwell! Consider other mitigations like masking indoors.

FAQ

Can I attend the virtual events if I'm not in Scotland?

Sure! But expect and be tolerant of conversation that is focused on Scottish places, events or news.

Will you record the events?

We might record the talks and questions but we will not record the community conversation.

Are the events free? How will this be supported?

The events will be free to attend. Later we may ask for donations or look for sponsorship to cover our costs. Teacake companies are particularly welcome.

Where will in-person events be?

They will be in Edinburgh, Glasgow and a few other cities - we are aware a Scottish event should cover more than just the capital!

Who is behind this?

Hello, I'm James Baster. I have experience hosting Tech Meetup Edinburgh, talking at events and running Open Tech calendar. Now I'm doing this event because I want something like it for myself! If you want to help out let me know.

What is the Code of Conduct?

This will be published before the first event.
